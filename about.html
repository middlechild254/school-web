About Mitaboni Boys High School

Shaping Future Leaders, One Child at a Time

Our Story

In January 2025, a group of 18 visionary individuals came together, united by a shared dream — to create a financial home where members could save, grow, and prosper together. Each founding member brought not only their contributions but also their hopes for a brighter financial future, one built on trust, cooperation, and empowerment.

From humble beginnings, the SACCO was born out of the realization that real change starts from within the community. Many of the founders had experienced the challenges of limited access to affordable credit and the struggle to achieve financial independence. They envisioned a solution — a member-owned cooperative that would nurture savings, offer fair loans, and uplift every member’s economic well-being.

The first meetings were held in simple settings — sometimes under a tree, sometimes in a borrowed hall — but always filled with determination and purpose. The 18 members agreed to pool their resources, contribute regularly, and build a sustainable institution rooted in transparency, accountability, and mutual growth.

As the months passed, the SACCO began to take shape: members started saving consistently, small loans were issued to support businesses and family needs, and confidence grew within the group. What began as a small circle of dreamers quickly became a movement of changemakers, proving that with unity and discipline, even modest beginnings can lead to lasting impact. Today, the SACCO stands as a symbol of hope, resilience, and collective progress. With a clear vision for the future, it continues to empower members, promote financial literacy, and strengthen the bonds that tie the community together.

Every contribution made to our SACCO represents a commitment to shared prosperity and sustainable growth. We remain dedicated to empowering our members through responsible financial management, innovative solutions, and unwavering integrity. Whether saving, borrowing, or investing, our Youth SACCO provides a reliable platform for young people to achieve their financial goals and build a secure future together.
