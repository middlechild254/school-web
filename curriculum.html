Mitaboni Boys High School Curriculum

Our curriculum fosters academic excellence, creativity and character development across all levels.

Curriculum Overview

At Mitaboni Boys High School we balance rigorous academics with hands-on learning, arts, and physical education to develop the child.

Early Years

Play-based learning that builds language, social-emotional skills and curiosity.

Primary Years

Strong foundations in literacy, numeracy and inquiry-based science and social studies.

Arts & Music

Creative expression through visual arts, music and performance throughout the year.

Sports & PE

Structured physical education, team sports and healthy living programs.

Our Teaching Approach

Inquiry Based

Students learn by asking questions and investigating real problems.

Differentiated

Lessons are adapted to meet each child's learning needs.

Technology

Safe, age-appropriate technology integrated into learning activities.

Assessment & Growth

Frequent feedback for students and regular parent conferences.
